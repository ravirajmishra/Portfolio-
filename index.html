Research · Hyperlocal Behavior

City Pulse — Offline to Online

🔴 Problem
Local Bangalore businesses had no way to measure how digital offers translate to physical footfall.
💡 Insight
Timing and urgency — not discount size — are the real behavioral triggers for offline store visits.
⚡ Decision
Ran a demand-routing experiment with real notification tests. Measured visit intent vs actual conversion.
✅ Outcome
Research findings now form the product strategy for a City Discovery app targeting tier-2 India.
What I'd do differently
Partner with 2–3 shops from day one instead of simulating — real data would have been stronger faster.
